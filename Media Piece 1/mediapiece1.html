Reflection Stop Motion

Reflection:

The following short film was created as part of my Intoduction to Digital Media module. It is a stop motion comprised entirely of cardboard sets and clay models. It tells the story of a young boy whose sombre outlook on life is changed when he meets a dog. The theme focuses an the inert human instinct to find happiness in even the darkest of circumstances.
